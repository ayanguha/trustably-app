Intelligent Applications¶

  • : Generative AI Solutions

    My Experience

    I have adopted GenAI application development fairly early. With strong fundamental understanding of Transformer neaural network models as well as embedding and vector databases, I have deep experience on popular libraries and toolings to deliver production-grade solutions.

    Specific Example

    I have worked deeply with my team to deliver an intelligent document processing solution. This solution is designed to process Insurance claims and extract information against original policy details. I have experimented in detail with various chunking techniques to deliver refined embeddings. I also experimented with different vector databases to determine right balance of semantic search and filtering techniques.

    This solution was delivered using AWS Lambda, AWS opensearch and langchain with langsmith framework.

  • : Machine Learning Solutions

    My Experience

    I have extensive experience in delivering traditional machine learning solutions. I have defined end-to-end processes for feature engineering, model training, deployment and inference pipelines.

    Specific Example

    I worked in Australia's one of the largest energy retailer to deliver a quarterly bill prediction use case for Small and Medium business customers. Leading the engineering team, I worked closely with Data scientists to identify electricity consumption patterns from smart meters across australia and convert important features to a timeseries metrics stream to train mofels regularly. I also worked to deploy the model via an inference pipeline such that entire SMB population is divided into multiple consumption-driven clusters and each new customer is assigned to its closest cluster - following a specific model trained for such cluster is applied during inference.

    This solution was developed in Spark and was deployed on Cloudera Hadoop platform.
